About Me

Welcome to my homepage! I am Kelum Chamara Manoj Lakmal Elvitigala, currently associated with Osaka University. My research focuses on the intersection of biotechnology and materials science, specifically in the development of hydrogels and their applications in tissue engineering and regenerative medicine.

Research Areas

1. Hydrogel Fabrication for In-Vitro Cellular Function Analysis

Fabricating hydrogels to analyze cell migration, cell growth, and cell cycle in vitro to better understand cancer cell behavior.

2. In-Vitro Angiogenesis Model Preparation Using Hydrogel

Developing hydrogels to replicate vascular environments for angiogenesis studies.

3. Cell Sheet Fabrication Using Hydrogel

Creating re-adhesive, differentiation-capable cell sheets using hydrogel for regenerative medicine.
