About Me

Welcome to my homepage! I am Kelum Chamara Manoj Lakmal Elvitigala, currently associated with the Graduate School of Engineering Science at Osaka University. My research focuses on the intersection of biotechnology and materials science, specifically in the development of hydrogels and their applications in tissue engineering and regenerative medicine.

Assistant Professor at Graduate School of Engineering Science and Global Center for Medical Engineering and Informatics, The University of Osaka .

  • Global Center for Medical Engineering and Informatics, The University of Osaka
